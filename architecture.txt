The machine consists of the following elements:

* Mechanical
 -Motors and gears to control the plate which follows the sun.
 -Casing to hold the azimut motor and the electronics
 -Plate with the sensors and shadowing device

* Electrical
 -Controller (ESP32)
 -Power supply (external)
 -Step up for motors (10-15V)

* Connector board
 - Connector for Powerbank
 - Step up for motors
 - Connectors for motors
 - Connectors for power supply
 - Stepper motor drivers
 - Connectors for interaction unit (display+control)

 * Controls
  - reset
  - on/off
  - wifi connect
